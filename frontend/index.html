Designed for Motor Skill Development

Handwriting is a complex motor task. For children with dysgraphia or physical learning difficulties, coordination can be frustrating. ScribbleMagic 3D turns practice into an active process that offers key advantages:

  • Active Muscle Memory: Large hand and arm gestures in the air (gross motor coordination) help reinforce neural pathways before transitioning to fine pen controls.
  • Multi-Sensory Feedback: Visually tracking the digital stroke as the hand moves strengthens spatial planning and finger placement confidence.
  • Stress-Free Environment: No erasing tears. Mistakes are cleared with a wave of a hand, keeping the kid motivated and smiling!

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about setting up and playing.

How does the camera drawing work?
The application accesses your webcam locally. Using computer vision models in the browser, it reads the coordinate of your index finger tip. Raising your finger draws a line, while raising both the index and middle fingers halts drawing and allows you to hover over control buttons.
Does it send my camera feed to any server?
No! The camera processing runs entirely inside your web browser (client-side). No video frames are sent to the backend server, ensuring complete privacy and speed.
Can I use this without a camera?
Yes, absolutely! Every activity supports normal mouse or touch interactions. If you don't enable the camera, you can draw and trace using your mouse or trackpad.
How does the AI predict my writing?
When you click the Predict button, the drawing canvas is captured and sent to the python server. The server runs a neural network for digits and a character reader (OCR) for words to predict what was written.
